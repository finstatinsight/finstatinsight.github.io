### Autocount Dotcom Berhad FY2025 Summary
Autocount Dotcom Berhad reported strong results in the first nine months of FY2025.  

- **Revenue:** <span style="color:#4CAF50">RM61,382k ▲ 34.81% YoY</span>  
- **PBT:** <span style="color:#4CAF50">RM37,477k ▲ 97.55% YoY</span>  
- **PAT:** <span style="color:#4CAF50">RM27,412k ▲ 90.93% YoY</span>  

The growth was powered by the **financial management software distribution segment**, demonstrating strong operating leverage and efficient cost management.

### Strategic Initiatives & Outlook
The Group is positioned to capitalize on:

- **Digitalization trends:** <span style="color:#4CAF50">high demand for automated financial management solutions</span>.  
- **Governmental support:** <span style="color:#03A9F4">RM50 million digitalization grant</span> for e-POS, CRM, e-commerce, and remote work.  
- **Strategic expansion:** <span style="color:#FF9800">regional growth funded by IPO proceeds</span>.  
- **Product innovation:** AutoCount e-Invoicing, OneSales PalmPOS, and cloud accounting programs with IAB LCCI.

Despite Q2–Q3 fluctuations, **strong cumulative 9M performance and clear strategic initiatives** position Autocount Dotcom Berhad for sustained growth in the digital finance sector.

### About Autocount Dotcom Berhad
[Autocount Dotcom Berhad](https://www.autocount.com.my/) provides **digital accounting, e-invoicing, POS, and cloud automation software**. Their solutions serve micro-SMEs, SMEs, and larger enterprises to improve operational efficiency and digital transformation capabilities.
